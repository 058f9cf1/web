DOCTOR WHO

All of time and space. Everything that ever happened or ever will. Where do you want to start?

    Season 1

    • Story 001

      An Unearthly Child

      After discussing their pupil Susan, two teachers follow her to a junkyard. They meet her grandfather, the Doctor, and force their way inside a police box.

    • Story 002

      The Daleks

      The travellers venture out on to the surface of a new world. Everything is dead, but beyond a petrified jungle they see a huge metal city which the Doctor is determined to explore...

    • Story 003

      The Edge of Destruction

      The TARDIS is suspended in space and the Doctor, Ian, Barbara and Susan are confused and frightened by a series of mysterious events.

    • Story 004

      Marco Polo

      The TARDIS lands in the Himalayas and the Doctor, Barbara, Ian and Susan meet Marco Polo and join him on his journey to Peking.

    • Story 005

      The Keys of Marinus

      The TARDIS lands on a remote island, surrounded by a sea of acid. They meet Arbitan, keeper of the Conscience of Marinus who forces the travellers to go in search of five keys.

    • Story 006

      The Aztecs

      The TARDIS lands inside an Aztec temple in 15th Century Mexico and become cut off from the ship when they leave the temple to explore.

    • Story 007

      The Sensorites

      The TARDIS lands inside a spaceship orbiting the planet Sense-Sphere. The ship's crew are apparently dead but surprise the travellers when they abruptly return to life.

    • Story 008

      The Reign of Terror

      When the Doctor attempts to return Ian and Barbara to their own time, the TARDIS actually lands in 18th Century France during the French Revolution.

    Season 2

    • Story 009

      Planet of Giants

      An accident in flight leads to the TARDIS and the travellers being miniaturised. Now only an inch high, the Doctor, Barbara, Ian and Susan explore an English country garden.

    • Story 010

      The Dalek Invasion of Earth

      The TARDIS lands in a ruined and empty London in the 22nd Century. Robomen patrol the shattered streets. The Doctor is eager to discover the cause of the city's plight.

    • Story 011

      The Rescue

      Arriving on the planet Dido, the Doctor, Barbara and Ian discover a crashed spaceship from Earth and survivors. They are terrorised by the monstrous Koquillion who attacks Barbara.

    • Story 012

      The Romans

      After relaxing at a Roman villa the Doctor is mistaken for a murdered lyre player and targeted by an assassin. Meanwhile, Ian and Barbara are captured by slave traders.

    • Story 013

      The Web Planet

      The TARDIS is pulled down to the barren world of Vortis. The Doctor and Ian explore. Barbara falls under an hypnotic influence and is compelled to leave the safety of the TARDIS.

    • Story 014

      The Crusade

      When the TARDIS lands in 12th Century Palestine, Barbara is captured by the Saracens. The Doctor, Ian and Vicki assist in saving King Richard. Ian is eager to go after Barbara.

    • Story 015

      The Space Museum

      Exploring a museum on the planet Xeros, the travellers discover they are invisible. The Doctor deduces that the TARDIS has jumped a time track and can see their own future.

    • Story 016

      The Chase

      Ian and Vicki are trapped by a tentacled creature on the planet Aridius. Meanwhile, the Doctor and Barbara witness Daleks set off on a mission to eliminate them!

    • Story 017

      The Time Meddler

      The TARDIS lands on the English coast in 1066, but the travellers are puzzled to find modern day artefacts. A monk observes the arrival of the TARDIS, but seems unsurprised.

    Season 3

    • Story 018

      Galaxy 4

      The travellers meet the Drahvins, a predominantly female race. Their leader, Maaga, has a crashed spaceship and evil plans.

    • Story 019

      Mission to the Unknown

      On the planet Kembel, Space Security agent Marc Cory uncovers a Dalek plot to exterminate humanity. Will he survive long enough to warn Earth of the Dalek threat?

    • Story 020

      The Myth Makers

      The TARDIS lands on the plains of Troy. Mistaken for Zeus by Achilles, the Doctor is taken to the Greek camp and meets Agamemnon and Odysseus who suspect him of being a Trojan spy.

    • Story 021

      The Daleks' Master Plan

      The TARDIS lands on the planet Kembel and the Doctor goes to find help for the injured Steven. He discovers that the Daleks are on Kembel and are planning to conquer Earth.
